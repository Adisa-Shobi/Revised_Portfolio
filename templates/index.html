Artvertise

Artvertise is a modern online marketplace built using cutting-edge technology like Express, React, MongoDB, and JavaScript. Our platform provides a seamless and user-friendly experience for artists and collectors alike. Its unique bidding system allows buyers to compete for their favorite pieces in real-time, creating an engaging experience for both buyers and sellers. It also offers auctions and curated collections, making it easy for collectors to discover new artists and styles. We utilize Passport for authentication, providing a secure and reliable way for users to log in and manage their accounts. To ensure that high-quality thumbnails are displayed for each piece of artwork, we use Sharp. This ensures that artwork is presented in the best possible light. We also use Multer to save image files, making it easy for artists to upload and manage their artwork. These packages and libraries, along with our state-of-the-art algorithms and advanced technology, create a platform that is both beautiful and functional. GitHub link
